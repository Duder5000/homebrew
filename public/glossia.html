Glossia

Glossia is a code first created by Inquisitor Gregor Eisenhorn. It works by taking the general idea that a word conveys, rather than it's literal meaning. As there is no code, it makes it far harder to crack. Here follows a list of words aproved for use by at least three Inquisitors (Nelson, Payne and Hannant):
   
Codenames
   Falcon: Inquisitor Christopher Nelson
   Lupus: Inquisitor James Payne
   Libra: Inquisitor Marcus Hannant
   Cyriacus: Joe Neale
   Hostis: James Cousins

Times
   The fires of Regicide: Break
   The gift of munificence: Lunch
   The Phoenix eternal: Club
   Final: The end of
   Foremost: The beginning of

Places
   The house of erudition: The library
   The Phoenix eternal: Club
   Court: The quad
   Underhand: Underneath
   Exceeding: Above

General
   Wishes: Requests a meeting with
   Multifarious: Chaotic, is chaotic
   Changelings: Servants of Chaos
   Imperative: Urgent
   Halflife: Danger

Patterns
   Pattern thimble: Make your way unobtrusively and in an unremarkable way
   Pattern vulture: Wait, and then move suddenly when the coast is clear
