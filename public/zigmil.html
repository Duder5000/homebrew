Breastplate of Kamvuul Norek

Armor (breastplate), legendary (requires attunement)
This adamantine breastplate of ancient Dhaakan belonged to a hobgoblin hero known as a mighty slayer of aberrations.
While wearing this breastplate, you gain a +2 bonus to AC, any critical hit against you becomes a normal hit<, and you gain advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects<.
In addition, if you succeed on your saving throw against the Mind Blast ability of a mind flayer or other aberration, you are unaffected, and the effect is reflected back toward the aberration as if the cone originated from you, including the aberration in its area of effect.

  • +2 AC
  • Adamantine (cancels crits)
  • Magic Resist (advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effect)

Blessing of the Queen

Necklace, rare (requires attunement)
This small wooden pendant has a long leather strap that adjusts to the preference of the creature attuned to it.
While attuned to this item you can cast the spell Guidance at will without any components. While cast in this way Guidance has a range of Self.
Once per long rest you can also hold the pendant to your ear and ask for advice. The advice will come in a form as if you had cast the Spell Augury.
While dreaming and you are attuned to this item you can attempt to commune with the Queen of Dreams.

  • At will Guidance (target self)
  • Once per long rest Augury
  • While dreaming you can attempt to commune with the Queen of Dreams
