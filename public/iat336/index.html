Material Ideas

I have 3 primary for the material of the body of the wallet. The first is just simple matte black plastic. The 2nd is a similar material to the first, but has texturing that makes it easier to grip. I'm not exactly sure what specific type of plastic it would be, but I would like something similar to the OnePlus Sandstone texture. The 3rd / exotic material is carbon fiber. Additonally, the wallet could have RFID blockers added into the material, possible only with the carbon fiber becuase of the added cost.

Lastly, I could use something as simiple as cup head bolt to connect the card holders or a pivot machined spefficly for it.
