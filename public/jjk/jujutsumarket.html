Cursed Compass

Grade 3 | $4000
This ornate compass doesn't point north. Instead, it vibrates and twitches in the direction of the nearest source of intense cursed energy. Grade 3 | $7500

Cursed Cell Phone of Resonance

Grade 3 | $15,000
This seemingly ordinary smartphone buzzes with subtle cursed energy. Its primary function is to amplify and transmit the user's cursed technique through its signal. When the user activates a cursed technique, they can choose to "broadcast" it through the phone, extending its range significantly (Double the Range). This can be incredibly useful for area-of-effect techniques or for supporting allies from afar. However, each use drains the phone's battery, which takes a long rest to fully recharge. It has 2 Charges.
After 3 Recharges the cell phone breaks from over heating.

Cursed Tea Set of Restoration

Grade 3 | $8500
This delicate tea set, infused with soothing cursed energy, can brew a restorative tea that heals both physical and spiritual wounds. Drinking the tea restores a number of hit points equal to the user's proficiency bonus + their Constitution modifier and grants advantage on their next saving throw against a disease or poison. The tea set can brew one cup per long rest. Along side this drinking this tea with a understanding of the soul lets you take this healing and make it Temporary Hit Points for your soul that do not go away till you take a long rest.
