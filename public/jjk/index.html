Cursed Techniques

  • Wire Manipulation

    A sorcerer born with the Wire Manipulation technique has the innate ability to create and manipulate thin wires. With expertise in this technique, a sorcerer can ascend to the mantle of Angel of Death..

  • Curse Grafter

    A sorcerer born with the Curse Grafter technique, has no innate abilities of their own, instead their body is able to accept grafts of curse spirits, granting them the spirit's power.

  • Royal Flush

    (WIP)

    A sorcerer born with the Royal Flush technique, can manifest a deck of playing cards made of cursed energy. Cards drawn from this deck grant the sorcerer different effects.

  • Glass Dancer

    (WIP)

    A sorcerer born with the Glass Dancer technique, has the innate ability to manipulate glass and, with practice, can enhance glass with cursed energy. Inspired by Brian McClellan's Glass Immortals.

  • Telekinesis

    (WIP)

    A sorcerer born with the Telekinesis technique, has the innate ability to push and pull creatures. Unfortunately, most users of this technique lack the prodigious skill to use it with much finesse. Over the years, sorcerers with this technique have learned to create specific Binding Vows to achieve the finesse they seek.

  • Dragon Scion

    (WIP)

    CT that allows the user to manifest parts of a dragon to attack them enemies, eventaully being able to turning to dragon completely.
    Based on Odvaskar's Dragon Scion fighter subclass.

  • Scorching Courage

    (WIP)

    A fire based CT that is party based on Ogi Zenin's Blazing Courage technique.

  • Limitless Redux

    (WIP)

    A partial rewtire of Limitless, that replaces Red & Purple with features based on only Blue, locking them till have the character has CTR.

  • New Shadow Style Redux

    (WIP)

    A mild rework of the New Shadow Style technique with the addition of a few new feats based on Simple Domains.

  • Hell Walker

    (WIP)

    A reversal or more like a devil version of Technique Extermination.

  • Tsubasa Clan

    (WIP)

    A yet to be named CT. However, here's the introduction description I wrote for it.
    "You were born to the Tsubasa Clan and you are one of the lucky members (~25%) of your clan to be born with the wing of a raven growing from your back."

  • Blood Manipulation Redux

    (WIP)

    A mild rework of the Blood Manipulation, plus some Kamo clan cursed tools.
