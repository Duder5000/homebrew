Spells

Spellscar Blade

Level:

1st

Casting Time:

1 Bonus Action

Range/Area:

Self

Components:

S

Duration:

1 Minute

School:

Evocation

Attack/Save:

None

Damage/Effects:

Fire

You draw forth the energy of your Spellscar to form a sword of blue fire in your hand. This magic sword lasts until the spell ends. It counts as a simple melee weapon with which you are proficient. It deals 1d8 fire damage on a hit and has the finesse, light, and thrown properties (range 20/60). Additionally, when you attack a creature that has already taken fire damage this turn, you have advantage on the attack (maximum of once per turn).
If you drop the weapon or throw it, it dissipates at the end of the turn. Thereafter, while the spell persists, you can use a bonus action to cause the sword to reappear in your hand.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a 2nd-level slot, the damage increases to 2d8. When you cast it using a 3rd- or 4th-level spell slot, the damage increases to 3d8, and has a +1 to attack rolls. When you cast it using a 5th- or 6th-level spell slot, the damage increases to 4d8, and has a +2 to attack rolls. When you cast it using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases to 5d8, and has a +3 to attack rolls.
