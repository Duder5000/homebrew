**Glyphs:** Magic, Life, Soul, Sorcerer, & Death *(sometimes in the book as Blood)*

After the characters step into the blue light and imagine a dragon made of blood bleeding into a humanoid vessel while the sigils of all spell schools glow brightly in the background. If they are not already thinking of their choosen destination on the Highway, they are promted to do so.

As they request assess to the High Sorcerer of Sanguinology's offices, they are blocked in turn by each of the glyphs. Once the final glyph has been passed they are teleported to the demiplane of the Sanguinologist.
